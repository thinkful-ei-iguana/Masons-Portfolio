About Me

I have always carried a passion in my heart for technology and discovery. My interest in coding specifically was sparked when I saw the movie “Hackers” as a kid, and it seemed like anything was possible with the right code. I have a scientific mind that embraces an order to things, and at the same time a creative side that ventures into the unknown for new and different possibilities. I look forward to working with great programmers because it will help expand on those unknowns, allowing me to see things from other perspectives that I wouldn't have thought of and producing even better results. It's fascinating to see how others handle a problem and work through a puzzle differently. Programming, to me, is an art form in which something is created from nothing and the possibilities are truly endless.


When I'm not writing code you can find me spending time with friends and family, traveling to new places, as well as going to music festivals and concerts. At home I enjoy listening to new music and playing guitar, discovering new shows, and playing games online with friends.
